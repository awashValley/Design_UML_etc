 –

 –

 –

(flowchart

 –

CATEGORIES
:
 
1)
 
Business

 –

(justification

•

•

Focus

of

value
 
of

2)
 
Constraints

development.
 
Type

procedures,
 
laws,
 
contracts;
 
technical
 
limits
 
like

legacy,
 
data
 
conversions,
 
data
 
types
 
and
 
sizes;
 
performance
 
limits
 
that

or

requirements.
 
3)
 
Rules
 
 –
 
where
 
constraints

like

Rules
 
should

to

is

is

 job
